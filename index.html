WHAT IS AQI:

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a numerical scale used to communicate how polluted the air currently is or how polluted it is forecast to become. It provides a way to understand and compare air quality in different locations. The AQI is often used by government agencies and environmental organizations to inform the public about the potential health effects of air pollution.
